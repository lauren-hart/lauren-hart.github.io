Have you tried using any of the insight and techniques for growing trust and sincerity? How did it go?

I was in a shop the other day looking around, the shopkeeper started talking to me and asked if I was having a good day. Usually I might brush them off with a quick response as I assume the only reason the are asking me about my day is to try and get a sale out of me.

Instead I assumed they were genuinely interested in how my day was going, so I kindly replied explaining that I am currently studying after quitting my established job. She seemed Genuinely interested and asked me questions and even said she admired me for what I was doing. It felt nice to open up a conversation with someone and treating them as a person just like me, rather than just a sales person with a hidden agenda.

Try empathic listening - how did it go?

I listened to a friend describe the difficult day he had at work today. He had a difficult case he was working on and missed a few important details before sending a document to the other party involved. He didn’t mention how it made him feel but I could see he was feeling like he wasn’t performing and it made him frustrated. I told him that he shouldn’t let it get to him and that everyone has days like that and I'm sure if he asked someone to take him through what he missed they would be more than happy to help. I tried to ignore the facts and focus on how it appeared to make him feel.

How did you find the loving kindness meditation?

It was strange thinking about someone you're so close to being so similar to you. Quite often you don’t think about what they have been through, which can be quite a selfish way of thinking. It is empowering to remember that no matter how different you are To someone, at the end of the day we all want the same thing, to be happy.

You can almost feel a warm feeling arise in your heart when you visualise the person and wish them to happy.

When a relationship ended in my past I was hurt and angry that I didn't hear from the person after it happened. It felt like they stopped caring completely when they should have still cared how I was feeling.

Now describe the situation as though the other person is (or other people are) 100 percent correct and reasonable. Do that either in writing or by talking about it in a monologue.

When the relationship ended I didn't contact them to see if they were okay. I really wanted to and it wasn't that I didn't care it was that I knew it would be harder for them to move on if I kept in contact in the short term.
