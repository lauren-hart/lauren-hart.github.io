What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

It is essentially the same thing. Being self aware requires you to focus the mind in the same way mindfulness does and to maintain a neutral mode whilst focused on the present moment with no distractions.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I tried the journaling activity. I found priming the mind before journaling an interesting technique and I actually felt like it opened up the flow of words a bit more in each instance. Thinking of an instance where I had responded positively put me in a good frame of mind and I felt I was more easily able to jot down things that gave me pleasure and my strengths. Same for the negative situation, it got me focusing on negative things.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

I found it very interesting what a difference priming the mind made for me. I really did notice an improvement in my mindset in the first part when it was about thinking of positive experiences, I also felt a lot calmer and happier. I would like to try and give this a go before going into a challenging situation and see if it helps how I address the situation.
