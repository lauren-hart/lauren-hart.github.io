phase zero

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I was conscious of the time spent on redesigning my site and at times I would get carried away in trying to perfect an area of my site. But I would break it down into small pieces and I was able to learn a couple of areas properly rather than trying to perfect the whole site redesign.
I struggled with the wireframes the most as I am not a good drawer and I a bit of perfectionist, so I spent a bit of time redrawing them. I still wasn’t happy with my final designs but had to move on to the next task and realise that I had given it a go and more time wasn’t going to increase my learning on the topic.

 What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I have dabbled in meditation and tried headspace a couple of times but have never stuck to it. I always though I was really bad at it because my concentration would always wander. I didn’t realise this was a good thing as it helps train our minds to come back to concentrating.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

Happiness is just being. It is not something that you pursue it is something you allow. Every time your brain wanders and you bring your focus back, it is training your mind.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

The grandma technique stood out to me - to see yourself in the eyes of a loving grandma.

I think I will adopt the pose of sitting up straight on a chair or bed with my feet on the floor and eyes closed (I am not flexible enough to sit cross legged with my back up straight).

Just acknowledging something is happening before reacting to it. Having five breaths before reacting to it.

Any other musings?

I found the concept of meta-attention fascinating - the ability to pay attention to attention itself.
