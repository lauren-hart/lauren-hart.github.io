daily mindfulness

phase zero

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I learnt from my mindfulness conversation that I don't listen very well. I have a tendency to try and finish people's sentences and I lose focus easily. It was really hard for me to sit and just listen for 3 minutes.

The other person enjoyed the conversation as it isn't often that he gets to talk for 3 minutes without any interruption from myself.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I will try the buddy technique. My boyfriend also tries to meditate so if we both hold each other accountable and encourage each other we will be more motivated to meditate regularly.

I think less is more will work well for me. I find more than 5 minutes quite difficult at the moment and I think by committing to only 2 minutes per meditation I will be more likely to stick with it.

Committing to one breath a day, if I can do this then I can work my way up to longer mediation periods.

Any other musings?

I like the idea of using joy as an object of mediation. I am going to focus on enjoying every meal I eat and being mindful of what I am eating.
